Inclusive Design

Introduction

Have you ever heard of the term "inclusive design" and what do you think it means?

people as possible with respect to one's capabilities, gender, age, religion, language,

and other forms of human differences. The importance of inclusive design is to raise equality

and allow everyone to access and use certain devices, software, tools, objects, and etc that they

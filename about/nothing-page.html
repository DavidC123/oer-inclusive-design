nothing about us
without us

“Nihil de nobis, sine nobis"

Latin for “nothing about us without us”; this means for a decision that includes a particular group, that group should be part of the discussion, included as a part of the process. While it is not to say the expertise of the other team members are not valuable but rather to have several or a group of people who have lived the problem is invaluable. A person can only assume and interpret the lives of those around them and as detailed and correct an interpretation may be it is not a first hand experience but a fabricated one built by filling in the blanks. The outcome is many products or services target a problem at surface level and gloss over the ‘small details’ either unthought of or deemed unimportant.
Such as the design of a prosthetic. Many prosthetics are made plain which bothers some children. The serious outlook reminds them of their difference among other students. Creating a more kid friendly design, such as an Iron man arm or one made out of legos, makes kids feel empowered. That they have something cool that other kids do not rather than one limb less.

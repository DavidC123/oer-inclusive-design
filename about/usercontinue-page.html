a usercontinued design

In a world with a population that takes more than a lifetime to count to (over 2 centuries if it is counted one number a second) there is an extremely diverse pool of people to cater to. Everyone varies slightly from one person to the next it is difficult to predict all the variations of people and circumstances. To cater to all needs, the user should have the ability to continue the design process to adapt the design to suit their needs. They should be able to undo, extend or even alter the design to suit their context, needs and preferences. For example, a user on Youtube can adapt and alter videos to suit their needs. Things such as video speed, captions and resolution can all be altered independently by the user. This makes the design more adaptable and ensures that it will cater to everyoneâ€™s specific needs.

a user-continued design

In a world with a population that takes more than a lifetime to count to (over 2 centuries if it is counted one number a second) there is an extremely diverse pool of people to cater to. Everyone varies slightly from the next person making it difficult to predict all the variations of characteristics and circumstances. To cater to all these different needs, the user should have the ability to continue the design process to adapt the design to suit their needs. They should be able to undo, extend or even alter the design to suit their needs and preferences. For example, a user on Youtube can change details such as video speed, captions and resolution. This adaptable design ensures everyone can enjoy their videos to the utmost comfort.
